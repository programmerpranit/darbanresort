RESTAURANT

Taste buds create memories and here at Darban Order whatever your Tummy wishes.

When you are in konkan its mandatory to pamper your tummy with sizzling konkani food. We serve a great variety of vegetarian and non vegetarian coastal Konkani flavors and Maharashtrian food. The authentic coastal Konkan flavours prepared with lot of care , sourcing the freshest seafood catch each morning, the most local ingredients from the area and each meal makes a memory. Trying Konkani cuisine is said to be an adventure in itself, as there is much to explore.

In addition to these, there are also a great number of vegetarian dishes as well. Many of these dishes tend to have coconut in them too, and this gives these dishes a characteristic taste that can be easily Recognized. Never miss our specialties like Fish fry, Curries and also multiple vegetarian options and our piping hot “Ukadiche modak” , made with fresh coconuts. The mouth-watering modak is one of the most loved dish from our restaurant.
